BIO

I am a professional artist who uses blacksmithing as my primary medium to create artwork. For the past two decades I have been creating custom iron and steel artwork for homes, individuals, and public art commissions in Tucson, Arizona. My passions in life are creating art and teaching. I am now sharing my enthusiasm for blacksmithing by instructing others.
